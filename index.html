Vessel Style Guide

Branding ideas

Vessels is a company that offers beautiful curated homeware that includes cups, vases, and bowls. The company is viewed by pontential customers as a place that has high quality products that exude a refined modernity and minimalistic good taste. Potential customers are enticed to purchase products because the branding of the site reflects the type of person they want to be and the lifestyle they want to live. It's meant to enhance the quality of both their identity and their home. Keywords: organic, refined, minimalistic, brutalist
